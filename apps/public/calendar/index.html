    J1 Calendar

    J1 Calendar is based on the time management solution provided by TeamUp. This cloud-based calendar software offers shared calendar for single persons or groups to simplify organizing, scheduling, and communication.

    The calendar is accessible via the Internet and simplifies how groups share plans, schedule events, and communicate statuses. Color-code calendars for team members, jobs, projects, and bookable resources. Centrally managed with highly customizable role-specific access permissions. No accounts are required.

    More information is available at the TeamUp Help pages.
